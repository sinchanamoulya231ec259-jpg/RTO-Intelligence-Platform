# 📦 AI-Powered RTO Intelligence Platform

## 🚀 Overview

The AI-Powered RTO Intelligence Platform predicts the probability of Return-to-Origin (RTO) for e-commerce orders before dispatch. The application helps logistics and operations teams identify high-risk orders and take preventive actions such as customer verification, prepaid payment recommendations, and address confirmation.

The project combines Machine Learning, Explainable AI (SHAP), and an interactive Streamlit dashboard to provide real-time RTO risk assessment.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Return-to-Origin (RTO) is one of the largest operational costs for e-commerce companies.

Common causes include:

- Cash-on-Delivery (COD) refusals
- Incorrect delivery addresses
- Long delivery times
- Low seller reliability
- Previous customer return behavior

Reducing RTO directly lowers logistics costs and improves operational efficiency.
